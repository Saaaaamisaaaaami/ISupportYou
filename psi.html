The Positive Support Initiative

The Positive Support Initiative aims to support cultural accessibility and inclusivity.

Positive support and cultural inclusivity. It's a complex topic. And a complex comparison. Our world is constantly changing, and when it comes to these two, no one really knows what to do when conflict arises. Through this initiative, we aim to understand the complexity behind these issues and comprehend the situation through the utmost sincerity that we can offer to keep our cultural iceberg afloat. We wouldn't want a Titanic situation anytime soon. Sorry. Bad joke.
