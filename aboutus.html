About Us

ISupportYou was started with the impression of creating a safe space for others. We are an online support organization dedicated to making every human being feel safe, happy, and loved. From a global perspective, fitness culture is gradually beginning to get accepted, and at ISupportYou, we are making sure that this awareness reaches everyone. Started by our founder, Samriddhi Chattopadhyay, in October 2024, we aim to bring these healthy lifestyles to light and help others understand the importance of balance. No matter what walk of life you may grace, we look beyond that and offer an extensive inclusive society. Our articles, initiatives, and chat pages are thresholds of creativity, diversity, and supportive content. Thank you for joining this website!
